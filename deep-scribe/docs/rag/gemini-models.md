# Google Gemini Models List (Jan 2026)

This document lists the available Google Gemini models as of early 2026, including Preview and Stable versions.

## Gemini 3 Series (Preview)

*State-of-the-art reasoning and agentic capabilities.*

* **`gemini-3.0-pro-preview`**: Latest reasoning-first model. Optimized for complex agentic workflows, coding, and adaptive thinking. 1M token context.
* **`gemini-3.0-flash-preview`**: Best for complex multimodal understanding and high-speed agentic tasks.
* **`gemini-3.0-pro-image-preview`**: High-fidelity image generation with reasoning-enhanced composition.

## Gemini 2.5 Series (Stable & Preview)

*High capability and efficiency balance.*

* **`gemini-2.5-pro`**: Advanced stable multimodal model. deep reasoning, code analysis. 1M token context.
* **`gemini-2.5-flash`**: High-volume, low-latency. Best balance of intelligence and speed for general applications.
* **`gemini-2.5-flash-lite`**: Optimized for massive scale and cost-efficiency.
* **`gemini-2.5-flash-live`**: Optimized for real-time bidirectional audio/video streaming.

## Gemini 2.0 Series (Legacy/Stable)

*Previous generation, reliable and widely supported.*

* **`gemini-2.0-flash`**: Cost-effective general-purpose multimodal model.
* **`gemini-2.0-flash-lite`**: Ultra-efficient for simple high-frequency tasks.
* **`gemini-2.0-pro`**: (If available legacy) Standard high-performance model.

## Gemini 1.5 Series (Legacy)

* **`gemini-1.5-pro`**: Long context window (up to 2M tokens), complex reasoning.
* **`gemini-1.5-flash`**: Fast, efficient, lightweight.

## Usage in Deep Scribe

These models can be configured in the **Settings** tab. The application defaults to `gemini-2.5-flash` for a balance of speed and intelligence.
